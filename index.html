Shortcuts & Guides
Tap photo → select  ·  Drag → move
Two fingers → scale + rotate  ·  Photo can span across slides
Arrow keys → move (Shift = coarse step)  ·  ⌘/Ctrl + / − → scale
Enter → fit photo into slide / frame
Format switch (top) picks Post 4:5 or Story 9:16 — switching resets the canvas
Snap guides while dragging: blue = center · red = edge · orange = align to photo · green = equal spacing
